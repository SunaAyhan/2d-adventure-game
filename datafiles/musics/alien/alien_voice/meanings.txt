1-) Your objective is here. Come to me. (Tvoya tsel zdes. Idi ko mne.) 
2-) Your path is coming to end. Come to me. (Tvoyo zhelaniye skoro ispolnitsya. Idi ko mne.)
3-) The time has come. I see your wish. (Preeshlo vremja. Ya vizhu tvoyo zhelanie.)
4-) Only one will be rewarded. (Voznagrazhdon budet tol'ko odin.)
5-) Your path has come to an end, human. Come to me. (Put' zavershon, chelovek. Idi ko mne.)
6-) Your wish will soon be granted. Come to me. (Tvoyo zhelaniye skoro ispolnitsya. Idi ko mne.)
7-) Come to me. You will get what you deserve. (Idi ko mne. Ti obretyosh to, chto zasluzhivaesh.)
